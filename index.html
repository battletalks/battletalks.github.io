Pizza

Ingredients

Dough

350g flour

25g dry yeast

1 teaspoon salt

2dl warm water

1/2dl oil

Fillings

Tomato soup

Pineapple

Fried mince meat

Cheese

Instructions

Mix flour, yeast, salt into a bowl. Mix oil and warm water, then add it to the bowl slowly, letting the water mix with the base. The base should become soft, uniform in texture blob that doesn't stick to hands when you knead it. Knead at the blob for 10 minutes, then put it into a bowl covered by cloth. Place the bowl into a warm place in the room. Wait a hour for the yeast to take effect.

After the yeast has inflated the base, knead it again for a while, press the air bubbles out from the dough. Divide the dough into pieces and stretch them few millimeters thick sheets. Put the dough above baking paper. Next add ingredients above the flattened dough. Tomato soup belongs to the bottom and the cheese belongs to the top, everything else between.
